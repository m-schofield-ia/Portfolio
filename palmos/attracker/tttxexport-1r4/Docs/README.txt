tttxexport
==========
 

tttxexport is used to export @Tracker archive files to another format.
Currently, standard csv, excel csv, php and debug formats are available.
tttxexport runs on Linux and Windows.


To un-install tttxexport (windows):

Use the "Add or Remove Programs" utility in the control panel.


Legal Stuff:

is free to use.  Look in LICENSE.txt for legalities.
