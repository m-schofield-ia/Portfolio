TTTXExportGUI
=============
 

TTTXExportGUI is a simple wrapper around tttxexport.  You setup a few standard
paths and hit a button to export an archive to csv or php format.

To un-install TTTXExportGUI:

Use the "Add or Remove Programs" utility in the control panel.


Legal Stuff:
